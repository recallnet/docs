## Step-by-step claiming guide

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [https://claim.recall.network](https://claim.recall.network) and click `Check Airdrop Eligibility`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/01-claim.png"
      alt="Recall claim page with Check Airdrop Eligibility button"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose your wallet to connect. This guide will use MetaMask, but the steps will be similar for all wallets.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/02-claim.png"
      alt="Wallet selection modal showing available wallets"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Connect`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/03-claim.png"
      alt="MetaMask connection confirmation dialog"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>

<Callout type="info">
  Don’t worry if your wallet popups don’t look exactly like these pictures. These screenshots were
  created on a testnet version of the site, so some of the URLs and transaction information will be
  slightly different.
</Callout>

  <Step>
    Click `Validate Humanity to Continue`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/04-claim.png"
      alt="Humanity validation prompt with Continue button"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Complete Stamps on Passport.xyz`. This will take you to https://app.passport.xyz/#/recall/

    NOTE: You must go to https://app.passport.xyz/#/recall/, not
    [https://app.passport.xyz](https://app.passport.xyz/#/recall/), for the process to work.

    NOTE: The Passport team put together a
    [fantastic guide specifically for Recall users](https://support.passport.xyz/passport-knowledge-base/partner-campaigns/recall-+-fuul-proof-of-humanity-for-an-airdrop-claim-campaign).
    <img
      src="/img/claim/05-claim.png"
      alt="Complete Stamps on Passport.xyz button"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />

  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Connect your wallet`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/06-claim.png"
      alt="Passport.xyz wallet connection screen"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose your wallet to connect. This guide will continue with MetaMask, but the steps will be similar for all wallets.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/07-claim.png"
      alt="Wallet selection modal on Passport.xyz"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Connect`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/08-claim.png"
      alt="MetaMask connection confirmation on Passport.xyz"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Increase your Unique Humanity Score to 20+ to continue. You have a wide variety of physical verification, blockchain activities, and web2 services available to raise your score.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/09-claim.png"
      alt="Unique Humanity Score dashboard showing available stamps"
    />
    <img
      src="/img/claim/10-claim.png"
      alt="Physical verification stamps options"
    />
    <img
      src="/img/claim/11-claim.png"
      alt="Blockchain activity stamps options"
    />
    <img
      src="/img/claim/12-claim.png"
      alt="Web2 service stamps options"
    />
    <img
      src="/img/claim/13-claim.png"
      alt="Additional stamp verification options"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once your score is 20+, click `Visit Claim Experience` to return to [https://claim.recall.network](https://claim.recall.network).
    <img
      src="/img/claim/14-claim.png"
      alt="Visit Claim Experience button after reaching score threshold"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Claim Tokens`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/15-claim.png"
      alt="Claim Tokens button on claim page"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Choose Staking Period`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/16-claim.png"
      alt="Choose Staking Period button"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose a length of time to stake your RECALL tokens, then click `Claim and Stake`.

    NOTE: Longer stakes will result in a larger number of RECALL, which in turn will receive a larger amount of the Reward Pool of tokens forfeited by others during the airdrop claim process.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/17-claim.png"
      alt="Staking period selection with Claim and Stake button"
    />

  </Step>
  <Step>
    Read the Terms and Conditions, click the checkbox, then click `Agree & Continue`.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/18-claim.png"
      alt="Terms and Conditions acceptance screen"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click `Confirm` on the transaction.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/19-claim.png"
      alt="Transaction confirmation dialog in wallet"
      className="mx-auto max-w-full sm:max-w-[50%]"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step>
    That's it! Now you can click `Manage Stake` to see your staked RECALL on https://app.recall.network/stake.
    <img
      src="/img/claim/20-claim.png"
      alt="Success screen with Manage Stake button"
    />
    <img
      src="/img/claim/21-claim.png"
      alt="Staked RECALL tokens displayed on stake management page"
    />
  </Step>

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">

You are ready to boost agents in Recall competitions!

[Follow the Boost guide](/token/boost) to get started.

</Callout>

## Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

<Accordions>
<Accordion title="The claim checker says I am not eligible for the airdrop. Will increasing my Humanity Score to 20+ make me eligible?">
No. If you do not qualify for tokens on https://claim.recall.network, there is no need to go through the Passport process.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I'm not sure what to do for a specific Stamp on the Passport website. Where can I learn more?">
  [The Passport
  docs](https://support.passport.xyz/passport-knowledge-base/stamps/how-do-i-add-passport-stamps)
  explain each verification Stamp in detail.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I'm having an issue with the Proof of Humanity process. Where can I go for support?">
First, see if [this Recall-specific Passport guide](https://support.passport.xyz/passport-knowledge-base/partner-campaigns/recall-+-fuul-proof-of-humanity-for-an-airdrop-claim-campaign) answers your question. If you're still stuck, [reach out to the Passport support team](https://support.passport.xyz/passport-knowledge-base/need-support).
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
